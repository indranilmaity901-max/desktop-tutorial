45. FOUNDER BRUTAL REVIEW 
This document completes a major missing dimension of WPACS: why someone would buy it. 
However, if I were acting as an investor rather than an architect, I would still challenge one 
assumption: 
The Biggest Risk 
Not technology. 
Not architecture. 
Not scalability. 
The biggest risk is: 
Can WPACS prove causation instead of correlation? 
Example: 
Agent = READY 
 
Workstation = LOCKED 
That is correlation. 
A customer will ask: 
Was the employee actually unavailable? 
 
Or was there a legitimate exception? 
The long-term success of WPACS depends on reducing false positives and building trust in the 
decisions it produces.
