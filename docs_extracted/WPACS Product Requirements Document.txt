1.1 Product Name 
WPACS 
Workforce Productivity & Activity Correlation System 
 
1.2 Product Vision 
WPACS is an operational intelligence platform designed to validate workforce productivity 
through multi-source state correlation. 
The platform collects operational signals from workforce systems and determines whether 
employee states align with expected business activity. 
The objective is to provide managers and executives with evidence-based operational 
intelligence. 
 
1.3 Mission Statement 
Transform operational assumptions into validated operational evidence. 
 
1.4 Product Goals 
Goal 1 

Provide real-time operational visibility. 
Goal 2 
Validate workforce activity using multiple sources. 
Goal 3 
Identify productivity conflicts. 
Goal 4 
Provide explainable productivity analytics. 
Goal 5 
Reduce manual operational investigations. 
Goal 6 
Improve management decision quality.
