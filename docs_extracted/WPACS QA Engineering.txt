Specification v1.0

Enterprise Quality Assurance, Validation & Release Governance Framework 
Document Purpose 
This document defines the quality strategy, testing methodology, validation framework, 
automation standards, release controls, and acceptance criteria required to ensure WPACS is 
production-ready. 
Audience: 
 QA Engineers  
 QA Automation Engineers  
 Developers  
 Product Owners  
 Release Managers  
 DevOps Engineers  
 UAT Teams  
 
1. QA PHILOSOPHY 
WPACS measures operational truth. 
Therefore: 
WPACS itself must be measurable. 
Every feature must be: 
 Testable  
 Repeatable  
 Auditable  
 Verifiable  
No feature enters production without validation.
