Evidence-Based Decision Architecture & Enterprise Trust Model 
 
1. DOCUMENT PURPOSE 
This document defines how WPACS proves, explains, justifies, and defends every productivity 
finding, conflict, alert, score, and recommendation generated by the platform. 
The objective is simple: 
No WPACS decision should require blind trust. 
Every conclusion must be supported by evidence. 
 
2. CORE PHILOSOPHY 
WPACS must never operate as: 
Black Box Intelligence 
WPACS must operate as: 
Transparent Intelligence 
Users must always understand: 
What happened 
 
Why it happened 
 
What evidence exists 
 
What rule triggered 
 
How confidence was calculated

45. FOUNDER BRUTAL REVIEW 
This document addresses the largest strategic risk facing WPACS: 
Trust 
A supervisor may look at a dashboard. 
A manager may review a report. 
But neither will consistently act on WPACS findings unless they believe: 
The evidence is accurate. 
 
The rules are fair. 
 
The conclusions are explainable. 
At this stage, the architecture set has crossed from a software concept into a credible enterprise 
product blueprint.
